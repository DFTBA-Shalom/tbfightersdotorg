END TB NOW ACT

Calling all US residents: the End Tuberculosis Now Act of 2023, which increases funding and sets goals to help end tuberculosis, just passed the Senate, and now we need to get it through the House of Representatives! Call your local Representative and tell them to vote YES on H.R. 1776! Go to this page for templates and resources to contact your Representative.
